Weight (kg) Estimation
  • Weight estimation based on age
    • Age is:
      • < 12 months:(kg) = (age in months + 9)/2
      • 1-5 years:(kg) = 2 x (age in years + 5)
      • 5-14 years:(kg) = 4 x age in years

    Ref: PubMed[2007]
